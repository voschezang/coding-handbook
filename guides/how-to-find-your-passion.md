# How to find your passion

> Passion follows expertise

The following steps would help to find your passion. It assumes you are healthy and have free time and energy. And a positive attitude (beginner’s mind).

1. Reflect on your life. See [life](../metaphysics/life.md).
2. Find a passion.
   1. Try different things to find out what interests you. **Gather data**.
   2. Gain expertise and develop a passion.
3. Explore your passion.
   1. Expose yourself to the passion. As a job, hobby or distant interest. 
   2. Expose yourself to jobs that relate to the passion
4. Develop the right skills and experience

See also [life](../metaphysics/life.md).



## Gathering Data

Gather data to discover what exactly is meaningful for you. Log emotions, inspiring moments, disappointments. Which events gave you energy, what was draining. Do this for a few weeks. Then continue for a few months.

For every event, note the kind of activity, the location, the objects involved and the type of interactions with people.
