# How to Solve Problems

This guide explores generic ways to solve all problems. It separates problems in two domains: obvious and complex. The first one doesn't really require a guide. See also the guides on [planning](how-to-plan-less.md) and [self-help](how-to-help-yourself.md).

## Obvious Domains

This is trivial. 

1. Recognize the problem type
2. Follow a template, runbook or recipe. Or follow your intuition.

E.g. when you see a red light🚦, break.

## Complex Domains

For complex domains. With complex risks.

1. 💡 Find the right problem. Accept unsolvable problems and solve other problems.
2. 📦 Find the right solution. Use prototypes.
3. ⚙️ Build the solution. Engineering, optimization.



Mindset

| Step                      | Mindset / Method                                             | Focus      |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------- |
| 💡 Find the right problem  | Design thinking, [systems thinking](../software-industry/systems-engineering.md) | Exploring  |
| 📦 Find the right solution | Lean startup, prototyping, bets                              | Validation |
| ⚙️ Build the solution      | [Engineering](../software-industry/engineering.md), project management | Quality    |

## Tools / Methods

Approaches

- ⚙️ Dive in. Deconstruct the problem like an engineer would.
  - "Write strategy documents

- ☁️ Zoom out. Put the problem into perspective. Change your attitude. Let a new solution reveal itself.
  - "Align to a vision"

- ⏸️ Pause. Do nothing.
